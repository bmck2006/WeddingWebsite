Our Story...

You've been there from the start for me. And your love's always been true as can be.
2013

August 9th, 2013: The First Date

Brian and Kayla met online, and hit it off immediately. After a few weeks of chatting online, they decided to meet for dinner and a movie. They saw "The Conjuring", which is still one of their favorite Horror movies today.

September 14th, 2013: Their First Trip Together

They went camping in the white mountains in New Hampshire. Hiking, sightseeing, relaxing, and of course the Alpine Slides at Attitash Mountain!

2014

July 22nd, 2014: Their First Concert Together

Brian took Kayla to see her favorite band, Avenged Sevenfold, at Mayhem Fest in Mansfield,MA.

2015

March 21st, 2015: They Adopt a Puppy!

Brian and Kayla decided to get a puppy. Widget was adopted from Southern Roots Rescue in Tennessee. She was driven up, and picked up in Connecticut.

Widget is a sweet, loving, ball of energy, and now a loved member of the family.

July 28th, 2015: They Move In Together

They move-in to their first apartment together, in Worcester, MA.

September 1st, 2015: They Adopt ANOTHER Puppy!

Tucson was another rescue from Tennessee. Kayla had been working in dog grooming at the time, and Tucson's previous owner brought him in to be groomed. Kayla discovered that he was up for adoption, and she and Brian had been looking for a brother for Widget.

2016

March 13th, 2016: He Proposed!

So how did Brian pop the question? On a balcony ... at the Breakers Mansion ... in Newport, RI!

At the end of the mansion tour, heading on their way down to the large backyard with incredible views, Brian got a "video message" from the dogs at home, on his phone. He played the video, got on one knee and popped the question!
